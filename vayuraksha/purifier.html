Purifier Specifications for the First Market Release

This page captures the concrete, near-term product details: what sensors are onboard, how reusable filtration works, and where the economics stand against traditional disposable-filter purifiers.

Smart Sensor Stack

The device continuously samples indoor air and surfaces actionable quality signals in the app and on-device panel.

PM2.5 Sensor Laser scattering module tuned for real-time particulate spikes, suitable for indoor dust and smoke events.
VOC Sensor Metal-oxide array estimating volatile pollutant load and trend direction for ventilation guidance.
CO2, Temperature, Humidity Comfort and ventilation context to prevent misleading purification-only interpretation.
Smart Alerts Threshold notifications, filter health estimates, and mode recommendations based on occupancy patterns.

Reusable Filtration System

The first product uses a reusable primary filter architecture to reduce replacement waste and lower recurring cost for households.

Washable pre-filter Captures larger particles and extends service life of downstream stages.
Regenerable core module Designed for periodic cleaning and controlled reuse cycles instead of frequent disposal.
Service rhythm Simple monthly maintenance check with app-guided steps and clear condition indicators.

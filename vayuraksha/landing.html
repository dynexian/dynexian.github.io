The Challenge

The Problem with Air Purifiers Today

For students in hostels and PGs, and for budget-conscious families, clean indoor air is often unaffordable. Premium branded purifiers come with high upfront costs, costly proprietary filters, and designs that are difficult or impossible to repair at home.

The result is simple: the people who need clean air the most are often the least able to access it.

High Upfront Costs

Branded air purifiers are too expensive for many students and middle-class households.

Proprietary Lock-ins

Replacement filters often lock users into costly proprietary ecosystems.

Low Repairability

Sleek commercial units are usually not designed for independent maintenance or part independence.

Unmet Needs

Many people want a clean-air study and sleep environment but lack an affordable option.

Strong Airflow

Engineered for brutal efficiency and effective filtration.

Standardized Parts

Uses standardized filtration components avoiding brand lock-ins.

Independent Maintenance

Easy replacement and maintenance designed for everyday users.

Local Sourcing

Durable construction with 90%+ local sourcing.

The Innovation

Our Solution: Project Vayuraksha

Project Vayuraksha is a modular clean-air system designed to make clean air more accessible. Instead of chasing expensive branding and closed ecosystems, it focuses on what really matters: strong air cleaning, low maintenance cost, locally available parts, and easy repairability. It is built around a practical, no-nonsense design.

In short, Vayuraksha is not a luxury gadget. It is a dependable clean-air tool for everyday users.
Mechanics

How Vayuraksha Works

Vayuraksha uses a modular system with standardized filtration and a high-static airflow setup to rapidly clean indoor air in a standard room. The design emphasizes practical assembly, reliable airflow, and easy component replacement instead of fragile sealed construction.

01

Standardized Intake

Air is pulled in through standardized, locally available, non-proprietary filter formats so replacement is easier and more affordable.

02

High-Static Airflow

A strong fan system drives high airflow to achieve strong clean-air delivery, clearing bad air, dust, and smoke faster.

03

Rapid Circulation

Cleaned air is circulated back into the room, creating a cleaner study, sleep, and living environment for users in small urban spaces.

04

Simple Maintenance

Because the structure is modular, filters and parts can be replaced with basic guidance and tools rather than specialized servicing.

Strategy

How the Business Works

Our model is strong because it does not depend on just one source of revenue. We utilize a simple but realistic structure built around product sales, repeat purchases, and institutional adoption, giving Vayuraksha both consumer relevance and small-scale B2B scalability.

Hardware Sales

Core revenue comes from selling the purifier unit at a practical hardware margin. This provides the foundational cash flow to scale operations sustainably without over-relying on subscriptions.

Recurring Filter Replacement

Optional bi-annual filter replacement subscriptions create repeat revenue while making maintenance easier for users.

Bulk B2B Sales

PGs, hostels, educational facilities, and housing complexes can place larger institutional orders.
